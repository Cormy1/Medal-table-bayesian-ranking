Research

Posterior Mean Rank

Uses the expected value of the posterior rank distribution from the Bayesian hierarchical model. Accounts for uncertainty and partially pools information across nations.

U-Index (DEA)

A data envelopment analysis-inspired composite rank that evaluates each country's medal efficiency relative to its peer group. Robust to outliers and covariate imbalance.

Non-parametric · DEA

Per-capita Rank

Normalises total medal count by population (in millions), giving small nations with outsized medal hauls — Jamaica, New Zealand, Norway — their proper credit.

Normalisation

Posterior Median Rank

The median of the posterior rank distribution, more robust than the mean to tail behaviour in the GEV prior. Useful when comparing nations at the extremes of the table.

GEV · Bayesian

Total Medal Rank

The baseline: raw medal total, ordered descending. Provided for comparison against the adjusted methods to illustrate where Bayesian adjustment shifts perceptions most.

Baseline

Covariates

Models incorporate country-level covariates: population, GDP per capita, life expectancy. Hierarchical priors allow partial pooling across editions of the Games.

WDI · World Bank
